# CarManager 🚗🚬

A cozy but shady used-car dealer game set in 1995 Belgium. Run your lot in
Essene at bus stop Koudenberg, buy cheap cars in Poland and Ukraine, drive
them home on ZZ transit plates, "prepare" them in the garage, and sell them
to unsuspecting Belgians. Don't let the heat get too high.

**Play it here:** https://roxorloops1337.github.io/carmanager/

Play as Martine (the CEO), Alan (the fixer) or Thomas (the charmer).
~95 real car models from the 50s to the 90s, a 3D showroom, odometer
"corrections", customs bribes, cigarettes and lots of Red Bull.

One HTML file, works on PC and mobile. Saves in your browser.
